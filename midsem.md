The following topics are included in the mid-semester examination:

    Regular Languages: NFA/DFA to Regex conversion is not needed.
    Non-regular Languages: Pumpable non-regular languages and Myhill-Nerode theorem are not needed.
    Context Free Languages: Ambiguity, Chomsky Normal Form, and PDA/CFG interconversion are not needed.


To prepare for the exam:

    Read the notes, slides, and book.
    Refer to the worksheets, and homework assignments on these topics for sample questions.
    It is recommended that you do the exercises from the textbook.
    Pumping lemma proofs need not be written as formally as in the notes/worksheets. However, they should be correct, legible, and coherent to get any points.
